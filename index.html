About

Crystal Fecteau

Hi, I’m Crystal Fecteau and I began my pastry career by baking for friends and family at the age of 14. I would design custom desserts for birthday parties, tea parties, and baby showers. I have always had a strong passion for baking and creating pastries while also being challenged to try new techniques. At the age of 18, I went to culinary school in Los Angles California and graduated two years later. After graduating I began to traveling to different areas in the US to work and build my career. I have traveled to Florida, San Fransico, San Jose, and now Seattle gaining new experiences along the way. I am now 26 years old and still enjoy creating new desserts for all different diets including gluten-free and vegan. This website is a small demonstration of what I accomplished over the years.
